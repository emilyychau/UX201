    Overview

    Our group followed the critical making process to achieve the goal of GMP1, to “hack” the concept of a box. We reimagined it as a multipurpose and fully functional light fixture, designed to address the need for affordable yet customizable pieces of room decor. It would help to support different aspects of students’ lives–including their mood, mental health, and productivity habits.

    Project Topics

    To “hack” the concept of a traditional cardboard box, reimagining its size, shape and configuration. We took into consideration the accessibility of the prototype and its construction using sustainably sourced (i.e. recycled, repurposed, already owned) materials.

    Aims & Objectives

    The aim was to create a box that provides storage, has re-usable writing surfaces, and incorporates ambient lighting.
